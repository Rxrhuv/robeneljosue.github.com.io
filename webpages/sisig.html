Pork Sisig

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pig ears
  • 1 lb. pig snout
  • 1 lb pork belly
  • 2 pieces onions (minced)
  • 3 pieces bay leaves
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 4 thumbs ginger (crushed)
  • 1 quarts water
  • ½ cup Lady’s Choice Mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons sukang iloko
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • ¼ cup liver spread
  • 2 limes
  • 1 tablespoon Knorr Liquid Seasoning
  • 1 egg

    • Procedures:

  • Combine pig ears, snout, pork belly, ginger, bay leaves, 2 teaspoons salt, and 1 quart of water in a cooking pot. Boil for 1 hour.
  • Remove from the pot and drain the water.
  • Heat-up your grill. Start grilling the pig parts for around 5 minutes per side.
  • Remove from the grill afterwards and let it cool down.
  • Make the dressing by combining all the dressing ingredients. Mix well.
  • Chop the grilled pig parts into small pieces.
  • In a large mixing bowl, combine the chopped pork with onion and the dressing mixture.
  • Toss until all the ingredients are well blended.
  • Transfer to a serving plate.
  • Add egg on top while sizzling
  • Serve with your favorite drink. Share and enjoy!
