Sinigang

Ingredients:

  • 500g pork belly or pork ribs, cut into serving pieces
  • 1 medium-sized onion, quartered
  • 2 medium-sized tomatoes, quartered
  • 2 cups of tamarind (sampaloc) or tamarind mix
  • 1 medium-sized daikon radish (labanos), sliced
  • 1 medium-sized eggplant (talong), sliced
  • 1 bundle of kangkong (water spinach), separated into stalks
  • 1 to 2 pieces green chili peppers (siling pansigang)
  • Fish sauce (patis) or salt, to taste
  • Water
  • Optional: other vegetables like string beans (sitaw), okra, or spinach can also be added according to preference

    • Procedures:

  • Boil the pork belly. In a pot, add the sliced pork belly and enough water to cover it. Bring it to a boil and let it simmer for 5-10 minutes until the pork is tender. Skim off the scum that rises to the surface to make the broth clear.
  • Add the vegetables.Once the pork is tender, add the sliced onions and tomatoes. Let it simmer for another 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are cooked.
  • Add the tamarind mix. Add the tamarind mix to the pot and stir well. Let it simmer for another 3-5 minutes.
  • Add the kangkong and green chili.Add the kangkong and green chili to the pot and let it cook for another 1-2 minutes until the kangkong is wilted.
  • Season with salt and pepper.Finally, season the Sinigang na Baboy with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot with steamed rice.
